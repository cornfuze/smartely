Herb Information

Asam Jawa

Tamarind Drink

Jamu Asam Jawa is a traditional Indonesian herbal drink made primarily from tamarind pulp, sugar, and water.

The name "asam jawa" refers to tamarind, which is the key ingredient in this drink.

Tamarind is known for its tangy, sour taste and is packed with nutrients, including vitamin C, antioxidants, and dietary fiber. The drink is typically enjoyed cold and is often consumed for its refreshing qualities, especially in hot weather.
